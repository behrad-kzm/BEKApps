● PlayCenter (Music Player, Downloader)


Play Center, a new player that you will enjoy playing music with.
With Play Center you can play your musics in one place, whether they’re stored in iTunes, Whatsapp, Telegram, etc.
You can even download musics from Youtube! Just open up the built-in browser, go to Youtube and click on videos you’d like, Play Center will download them so you can play them offline.
Play Center has an intuitive and smooth user interface that you would love working with.

• Manage songs from all installed programs such as iTunes, Telegram, Whatsapp, Note and ...

• Import and save songs through iTunes software.
• Compatible with Car players.


● Autoreply pal (Background Location Tracker)


The AutoReply Pal is an IOS application that track user location in background mode. It can reply to an specific email with specific subject that is pre-defined by the user through the user interface or remotely via trusted user. The app doesn't use any private libraries and every thing developed legally.
Developed "Autoreply Pal" iOS application in Objective-C that tracks device location in the background. It can send tracking details via email to the configured recipients.

Bullet Points:

• Developed a location manager with significant low battery usage(less than 2% battery usage per 5 hours). The component starts automatically running after a phone reboot and with no dependency on third-party libraries.
• Integrated Gmail API with the app for sending and receiving emails.
• Developed a very accurate alert manager to notify configured recipients of entering the mobile device to predefined location zones.Working with Realm database to store locations, alerts, contacts, etc.
• Used Crashlitycs, Onesignal, Flerry, Mailcore-2, AFNetworking-3, Realm.
